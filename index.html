Cheeseburger

Description


A nice American beef patty served on a whole wheat bun with Wisconsin cheese, homegrown tomatoes, and condiments of your choice.

Ingredients

  • 6 oz Ground Chuck
  • 1 Whole Wheat Bun
  • 2 slices of Wisconsin Swiss Cheese
  • 3 slices of Heirloom Tomatoes
  • Ketchup, Mustard, Relish, etc.

Directions

  1. Heat grill to medium heat
  2. Press patty into proper and uniform shape
  3. Lightly salt, pepper, and season until satisfied
  4. Toss that patty on the gril and cook until medium rare
  5. 30 seconds before removing the patty from the grill, add your cheese to let it melt on
  6. Place the patty on the bun, add the condiments of your choice, and serve immediately
